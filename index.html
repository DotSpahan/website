About Imc':

Imc' Photography's name "Imc'" comes from the Nlekepmx language and means "grandchild" and sounds like the english word "Image" Owner/Operator, Dorothy "Dot" Spahan, from Quinsahtin Creek, origin of the Stuxix-Athapascan ancestry has strong grassroots background. Growing up on the land, cultivating and harvesting food and medicine off the land with her grandmother, great-grandmother and great-great grandmother of the Nlekepmx Nation.
